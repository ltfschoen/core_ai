Core Sample Analysis

Easily get instant analysis of your drill core sample photos

We provide you with in-field app that identifies structural features in core rock samples:

  1.    Detects features on a drill core segment using algorithms
  2.    Projects selected features into 3-Dimensions
  3.    Reports orientation as a stereograph

Step 1: Processing

Click this button to select a core sample image from your device and crop its dimensions.

  

Step 2: Simplification, Detection, and Presentation

After cropping your image, click this button to upload it and and we'll detect characteristic shapes (ellipses) of any defects.

  

Step 3: Review Results

Review analysis results and fine-tune.
